PkmnQuiz

Privacy Policy

Effective June 16, 2026

PkmnQuiz does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Information we collect

None. PkmnQuiz does not ask for, gather, or receive any personal information. We do not use accounts, sign-in, advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or any third-party tracking services.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of gameplay data — your best scores and your flashcard learning progress — locally on your device so it persists between launches. This information never leaves your device, is never sent to us or any third party, and is removed if you delete the app.

Network use

PkmnQuiz works entirely offline. All content is bundled within the app, and it makes no network requests.

Children's privacy

PkmnQuiz is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children. No personal information is ever requested or processed.

Third parties

PkmnQuiz does not integrate any third-party services, share data with anyone, or sell information. There is no data to share or sell.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
