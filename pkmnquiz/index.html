Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Tap Start Quiz, then type names to reveal each silhouette before time runs out. Turn on Spelling Help in Settings if you want close guesses accepted.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your best scores and your flashcard "known" progress are stored on your device and persist between launches.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Everything is bundled in the app — no internet connection, account, or sign-in is ever required.

What languages are supported?

English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, and Japanese. You can also choose which language to answer in from Settings.

How do I change the timer or duration?

Open Settings on the home screen. You can switch between count-up and countdown, and pick the minutes per game.

What's Study mode?

A no-pressure way to learn: flip flashcards with active recall (cards you miss come back around), or browse the full set as a labelled grid.

Privacy

PkmnQuiz does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. All progress stays on your device.
