Tomawatchi Privacy Policy (Last updated - 3/10/15) Welcome to Tomawatchi ("Tomawatchi," "we," "us" or "our"). Tomawatchi is a playful application to promote physical exercise through step counting. Tomawatchi does not collect your data. We simply read the step count that your device has. We do not store or share any of your health information with any third parties. All health data is stored locally on your device.
